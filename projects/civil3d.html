Surface Creation for DEM

In this project, I generated accurate Digital Elevation Models (DEMs) using survey data. Civil 3D was used for terrain modeling, grading, and surface analysis to support hydropower and infrastructure projects.

Key tasks included:

  • Importing survey and drone point data
  • Creating TIN surfaces and contour maps
  • 3D modeling for design visualization
